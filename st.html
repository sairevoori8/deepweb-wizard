Student Council

The college has an autonomous student council that includes all registered students. One reviewer says the student council is enthusiastic and helpful.

Roles and Responsibilities

The Student Council plays a vital role in representing the interests and concerns of the student body. Some of its key roles and responsibilities include:

  • Advocating for student needs and concerns to the college administration.
  • Organizing and coordinating various student events and activities.
  • Facilitating communication between students and faculty.
  • Providing support and resources to student clubs and organizations.

Achievements

Over the years, the Student Council has achieved several milestones, contributing to the vibrant and inclusive campus environment. Some notable achievements include:

  • Successfully organizing large-scale campus events that promote unity and collaboration.
  • Implementing initiatives to enhance student well-being and academic experience.
  • Establishing effective communication channels to gather student feedback and address concerns.
